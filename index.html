About EMA

Quietly precise. Deeply personal.

Estate Management Advisors supports private households, principals and family offices in the appointment of exceptional private-service professionals.

Every engagement is shaped around the household itself: its rhythm, standards, complexity, culture and long-term vision. We advise on role design, leadership needs, market positioning, candidate evaluation and placement strategy.

Our approach is selective by design. We value judgment over volume, discretion over visibility and long-term fit over short-term speed.

Founder Perspective

Private service is about more than experience.

It is about judgment, trust, discretion and understanding how a household truly operates.

Andrew Urbain founded Estate Management Advisors to bring a more thoughtful, informed and operationally grounded approach to private-service search.

With more than 19 years of experience serving some of the world’s most prestigious family offices, Andrew has built, managed and supported multidisciplinary teams across complex principal and estate environments. His background includes senior leadership in estate and property management, real-estate acquisition, large-scale construction and renovation projects, budget oversight, workforce planning and vendor procurement.

Previously, Andrew served as a Senior Manager within a globally prominent family office. That experience gave him a firsthand understanding of what exceptional private service requires: discretion, judgment, operational discipline, adaptability and the ability to earn trust over time.

Estate Management Advisors was created around a simple principle: the strongest placements are not defined by credentials alone. They succeed because the individual understands the household, the principal, the culture and the expectations behind the role.

“The right person does more than perform the role. They understand the household, anticipate its needs and strengthen the environment around them.”

“EMA understood not only the role, but the household behind it.”
